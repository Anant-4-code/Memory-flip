2. What Information Do We Collect?

Information automatically collected:

When you play Memory Flip, we may automatically collect certain information. This information does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information, such as your IP address, browser and device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, device name, country, location, information about how and when you use our game and other technical information. This information is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of our game, and for our internal analytics and reporting purposes.

Specifically, our game utilizes browser localStorage to save your current game level and highest level achieved. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use the information we collect or receive:

  • To save game progress: We use localStorage to save your game progress (current level and highest level achieved) directly on your device. This allows you to resume your game from where you left off.
  • For analytics and improvements: We may use automatically collected information for data analysis, identifying usage trends, and to improve our game.

4. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties for their promotional purposes.

The game progress information (current level, highest level) is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

5. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

Since we primarily store game progress data locally on your device via localStorage, the security of this data largely depends on the security of your device and browser. We do not have access to this data remotely.

6. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

Since we do not collect personal identification information, your primary control over the data (game progress) is through your browser's settings. You can clear your browser's localStorage to remove any game data stored by Memory Flip.
